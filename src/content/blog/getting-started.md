This is the first post on what I hope becomes a useful personal record.

I work in procurement in the UK and there's a lot to keep track of — regulations, frameworks, supplier relationships, contract management, and the day-to-day reality of making it all work. I've found myself rediscovering the same things repeatedly, so this site is an attempt to fix that.

My plan is to write up things as I encounter them: what I learned, what surprised me, what I'd do differently. No fixed schedule — just when something's worth recording.

Topics I expect to cover:

- UK procurement regulations and reform (the Procurement Act 2023 in particular)
- Contract management and supplier relationships
- Framework agreements and how to use them well
- Day-to-day process and lessons from experience

If you've found this, welcome. If you spot something wrong, I'd genuinely like to know.
